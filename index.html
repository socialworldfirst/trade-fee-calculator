Exchange rate markup

Bank FX markups are estimated by comparing published bank exchange rates against the mid-market rate (the midpoint between buy and sell rates on global currency markets). The ranges shown represent typical retail/SME rates — corporate accounts with negotiated pricing may receive tighter spreads.

WorldFirst rate

WorldFirst charges a maximum of 0.6% FX markup on major currency pairs. Local-to-local transfers (e.g. CNY to CNY) carry no wire fee. The actual rate may be lower depending on currency pair and volume.

Wire fees

Bank wire fees shown are typical SWIFT international transfer charges including the bank's own fee plus standard SWIFT network charges. Receiving bank charges (if any) are not included for either provider.
