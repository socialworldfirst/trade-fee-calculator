Methodology

Each corridor researched across 7 cost dimensions: FX markup, transfer fees, intermediary/receiving fees, account costs, speed, safety/regulation, and limitations. Providers include domestic banks (top 3–5 by market share), fintechs (Wise, Airwallex, OFX, XE, Remitly, plus regional specialists), and WorldFirst.

WorldFirst costs use the maximum published FX ceiling from the WorldFirst Product Handbook — not typical or negotiated rates. Bank costs use standard retail/SME online banking rates. All data points sourced with URLs and access dates.

Currency composition researched per corridor. Reports include USD Route and CNY Direct comparison where applicable. Decision frameworks note where WorldFirst is not the cheapest option.

Cost estimates based on published rates and independent comparison data as of April 2026. Actual costs vary by provider, account type, relationship, transfer amount, and market conditions. WorldFirst costs use maximum published FX ceilings per corridor. This research is for illustrative purposes and does not constitute financial advice.
