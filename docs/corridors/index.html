10 providers · 5 banks, 4 fintechs, WorldFirst · April 2026

Most expensive corridor. Double conversion structural (TRY→USD→CNY). Thinnest fintech market — Remitly, WorldRemit, XE, OFX don't serve Turkey outbound. ICBC Turkey CHINEX is the hidden gem (59 TRY flat, same-day). RMB clearing bank launched Nov 2025.

Methodology

Each corridor is researched across 7 cost dimensions: FX markup, transfer fees, intermediary/receiving fees, account costs, speed, safety/regulation, and limitations. Providers include domestic banks (top 3-5 by market share), fintechs (Wise, Airwallex, OFX, XE, Remitly, plus regional specialists), and WorldFirst.

WorldFirst costs use the maximum published FX ceiling from the WorldFirst Product Handbook — not typical or negotiated rates. Bank costs use standard retail/SME online banking rates. All data points are sourced with URLs and access dates.

Currency composition is researched per corridor to determine the payment route split (USD vs CNY vs local currency). Each report includes a USD Route and CNY Direct comparison where both currencies exceed 20% settlement share.
